FAQs

  1. Will there be a demo day where the startups will be required to showcase their solutions?
    Yes. The demo will be scheduled in the month of April, date for which will be announced shortly. Tentatively, this would be in the 2nd half of April 2018
  2. What will be the location for the demo day?
    The Demo day will be conducted in Mumbai
  3. What would be the location of the Proof of Concept (PoC)?
    The PoC will be carried out in Vizag, Andhra Pradesh
  4. Will the PoC be paid for?
    This will entirely depend on the negotiations between the shortlisted startups and the corporates
  5. If a shortlisted startup prefers to perform the PoC from their own base office instead of Vizag, will that be accepted?
    No. As, the location for the PoC being Vizag is a mandate for the program, the POC would necessarily have to be executed from Vizag. Office space will be provided for operations in Vizag. A startup having difficulty to work out of Vizag may have to forego their offer
  6. Will the PoC contract be between the startup and Andhra Pradesh government?
    The PoC contract will be between the startup and the corporates only
  7. What will be the duration of a PoC?
    The duration of the PoC will be between 30- 45 days depending on the scope
  8. What happens if the PoC is not completed in 45 days?
    The extension will be given on a case to case basis
  9. Will there be a full scale implementation post completion of a given PoC?
    At discretion of the associated corporate partner, there could be a full scale implementation project offered to the startup involved. However, this will not be part of the initial contract and will be assessed only after completion of the PoC
  10. Who should participate?
    Any startup, which has capabilities to address a given use case through their solution offerings
  11. Is there a registration fee?
    No, there is no registration fee
  12. Does registering for the event ensure participation for the Demo day?
    No. Once the startups have applied for the event, they will be screened by an internal panel. Post the screening, the shortlisted startups will be invited for the event
  13. What will be the criteria for Evaluation?
    The startups will screened based on 3 parameters viz., Solution Fitment, Product Maturity, Team Credentials
  14. Is there reimbursement for travel/accommodation?
    No reimbursement for travel or accommodation will be provided
